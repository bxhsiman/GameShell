次要目标
-------------------

理解 ``cd -`` 和 ``cd ..``的不同


秘籍
===============

cd -
  跳转到你之前移动到的位置
  Jump back to the location you were in prior to your last move.

cd ..
  移动到当前目录的上级目录(沿当前路径后退一步)
  Move to the parent directory (one step back along the path to your 
  current location).

pwd
  查看当前位置
  See the path to your current locatio
